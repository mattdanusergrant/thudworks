## Why it won

`thudworks.com` was the only contender with **no judge below 7** — the slang names spike with the
Beatmaker but crater with the Strategist and Cynic (current slang + `.net` = short shelf life). It's
coined and ownable (clean search + type-in), `.com`, ages well (no slang to rot), and "Works" leaves
room to grow beyond a beat toy. `makedopegrooves.com` placed 2nd almost entirely on J4's brand-synergy
argument — which is why it survives as the **tagline** rather than being discarded. The merge captures
both Operator favorites at the levels where each is strongest: thudworks as the brand, "make dope
grooves" as the line beneath it.
